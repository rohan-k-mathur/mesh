# History and Lineage

> Where the programme comes from, in plain prose. This file is the programme's
> intellectual autobiography. It is updated annually, not quarterly.

## Inheritances

The programme stands on six lineages:

1. **The Girardian Ludics line** — Girard's *Locus Solum* (MSCS 2001) opened
   the substrate; Faggian & Hyland (CSL 2002) gave us the DDS reformulation
   the engine uses; Terui (TCS 2011) gave us computational Ludics; Fouqueré
   & Quatrini (LMCS 2013) gave us the uniqueness-of-incarnation result that
   the cone decomposition (T002) depends on; Baelde, Doumane, Saurin (CSL
   2015/2016/2017) gave us internal completeness and the fixed-point work
   for `μMALL∞` which informs (without dictating) our treatment of
   behaviours as directed systems.

2. **The dialogue-Ludics line (French ANR PRELUDE, ~2006–2009)** — Lecomte,
   Quatrini, Tronçon, Fleury, Fouqueré. We inherit the program of applying
   Ludics to natural-language dialogue; we depart at Reading C
   ([Q-002](01_OPEN_QUESTIONS_REGISTRY.md#q-002), [C002](03_CONJECTURES/C002-reading-c-conservative.md)),
   which no paper in that tradition adopts.

3. **The Lorenzen / Lorenz / Felscher / Krabbe dialogical-logic line** —
   the original commitment to meaning-as-dialogue. We inherit the picture
   but reject the single-protocol commitment.

4. **The Hamblin / Mackenzie / Walton / Walton-and-Krabbe / Prakken
   commitment-store dialogue-game line** — typed moves, commitment stores,
   protocol-relative legality. We inherit the apparatus; we depart on the
   *flat* treatment of commitments, replacing it with locus-stratified,
   witness-bound commitments (Q-010).

5. **The Dung / Caminada / Modgil & Prakken / Bench-Capon abstract
   argumentation line** — abstract argumentation frameworks, extension
   semantics, ASPIC+ as structural bridge. We inherit AF as the *coarse*
   semantic layer and use ASPIC+ for the structural bridge; we depart by
   making the argument's internal geometry primary and the AF view a
   projection.

6. **The Ambler / Krause / categorical-argumentation line** — Ambler 1996
   (MSCS), Krause–Ambler–Elvang-Gøransson–Fox 1995. We inherit the
   semilattice-enriched CCC framing of argument hom-sets. The bridge to
   Ludics ([C001](03_CONJECTURES/C001-ambler-bridge-iso.md)) is original to
   this programme; the two literatures have not previously cited each
   other.

## Adjacent debts (not lineages but resources we depend on)

- **Brandomian inferentialism** for the picture of meaning as inferentially
  articulated. We use it as a regulative frame, not as a technical input.
- **Sellars's logical space of reasons** for the refusal of the Myth of the
  Given; we add the Plexus pluralisation (the space of reasons is a graph
  of rooms, not a single space).
- **Habermasian discourse ethics** for the legitimacy framing; we treat the
  procedural conditions as testable infrastructure rather than as a
  regulative ideal.
- **Social epistemology (Goldman, Mercier & Sperber, List & Pettit,
  Fricker)** for the framing of epistemic systems as designed artefacts;
  we adopt the argumentative-function hypothesis as a working empirical
  conjecture (feeds [S003](04_EMPIRICAL_STUDIES/S003-mcp-ai-vs-human-authorship.md)
  and Q-009).
- **Walton's argumentation schemes** for the operational taxonomy. We use
  the schemes nearly as Walton states them; the divergence is in
  *constitutive* vs *diagnostic* status (see 00_CHARTER §4).

## Disagreements we own

- Reading C as the right multi-agent reading of Ludics, against the
  uniform Reading A of the dialogue-Ludics tradition.
- A *constitutive* (not diagnostic) reading of Walton schemes, against
  Walton's own framing.
- The argument-graph (and not the AF) as the primary semantic object,
  against the Dung tradition's representational asceticism.
- Stratified, witness-bound, locus-grounded commitments, against the flat
  propositional commitments of the Hamblin/Singh tradition.
- "Consensus" as structurally definable in the substrate (an open
  philosophical question, [Q-009](01_OPEN_QUESTIONS_REGISTRY.md#q-009)),
  against the deliberative-democracy tradition's reluctance to give
  consensus a fully formal characterisation.

## How the programme came to be (one paragraph)

The programme is a re-framing of work that had already accumulated as
scattered design artefacts. Two literature rounds
([`05_LITERATURE/INGESTION_LOG.md`](05_LITERATURE/INGESTION_LOG.md))
pressure-tested 25 falsifiable claims and located the gaps the substrate
was uniquely positioned to fill. The Phase 2e refutation of the original
OQ-JSL claim, and its Phase 2f reformulation as a per-cone result, made it
clear that the project was producing real theoretical work whose surface
had no proper home. This directory is that home.
