## Setting

The **Plexus claim-sheaf** over the room cover. Fix a claim to track. 0-cells of the **compatible nerve** $N$ are rooms; 1-cells are **pairwise-compatible** room pairs $\{r,s\}$ — those whose transport functors form a faithful back-edge 2-cycle returning the tracked claim to an **ECC-inter-derivable** claim (equivalently: the pair is in $\mathcal{P}^\circ$ as a 2-cycle, T010). A **local section** assigns the tracked claim its room-local identity; **restriction** along a 1-cell is the claim-map; the **monodromy** of a cycle of $N$ is the round-trip claim-map composite.

Possibilistic ($\check{C}$ech, **boolean / min-quantale** coefficient — Session 07 §2.2) cohomology: $H^0$ = global sections (a consistent tracked-claim identity across all rooms), $H^1$ = the obstruction to extending compatible local sections to a global one.

## The conjecture

> **C015 (possibilistic $H^1$ = iso-monodromy obstruction).** For the Plexus claim-sheaf over the compatible nerve $N$:
> 1. **(support).** A non-trivial $H^1$ class is carried **exactly** by a cycle of $N$ — every edge a pairwise-compatible 1-cell — whose **monodromy is non-trivial** (the tracked claim does **not** return up to ECC iso). Equivalently, by [Session 08 §5](../10_IDEATION_SESSIONS/08-distributed-semantics-quantitative-cohomology-2026-06-08.md)'s partition, exactly the **globally obstructed** cycles.
> 2. **(coboundary-triviality of local obstructions).** A **locally obstructed** cycle (some edge is a drop or a non-faithful pair, hence **not** a 1-cell of $N$) carries **no** $H^1$ class: it is not a cycle of $N$, and re-choosing the local section at the bad vertex removes it (a coboundary).
> 3. **(identification).** Therefore the **Abramsky contextuality obstruction** of distributed deliberation — pairwise-consistent local sections that do **not** glue — *is* the **iso-monodromy obstruction** isolated by [T010](../02_THEOREMS_AND_PROOFS/T010-plexus-coherence-pseudofunctor.md): $H^1$ is supported exactly on the cycles **outside $\mathcal{P}^\circ$ that are nevertheless cycles of $N$** (pairwise in, globally out).

The headline reading: *"the disagreement is a non-trivial cohomology class"* $=$ *"this is a real, irreducibly global disagreement — pairwise everyone agrees, yet there is no consistent global position"*, and that class is computable from the existing claim-map + ECC-iso data with **no schema change**.

## Positive settlement (what a proof / mechanization looks like, and where it goes)

1. **The degree statement (corroborated; rank argument LANDED 2026-06-08).** Parts (1)+(2) are demonstrated by the `cech` construction ([Session 08 §5.2](../10_IDEATION_SESSIONS/08-distributed-semantics-quantitative-cohomology-2026-06-08.md)) on the synthetic witness ($\beta_1(N)=1$, $H^1=1$, off-nerve local drop $\to 0$). **The rank argument is now a genuine cycle-basis / holonomy-group computation** (`computeH1RankBasis` in [`scripts/plexus-topology-probe.ts`](../../scripts/plexus-topology-probe.ts)), replacing the earlier "distinct-room-set proxy capped at $\beta_1$":

   > **Rank lemma (holonomy-group form).** Over a connected component of the compatible nerve $N$, pick a spanning tree rooted at $r_0$ and propagate each root claim's section along the tree. Each of the $\beta_1(N)$ non-tree edges gives a fundamental cycle whose holonomy is a permutation $\sigma_e$ of $r_0$'s surviving claim-iso-classes. Let $G = \langle \sigma_e \rangle$ be the **holonomy group**. Then the possibilistic $H^1$ obstruction is non-trivial iff $G \neq 1$, and
   > $$\operatorname{rank} H^1 \;=\; \log_2 |G| \;=\; \beta_1(N) - \dim_{\mathbb{F}_2}\ker(\text{holonomy } 1\text{-cochain}),$$
   > **exact when $G$ is an elementary abelian 2-group** (all holonomies involutions — the binary $p/q$ case); a strict lower bound otherwise (the probe flags non-involution $G$).

   **Why this is a real improvement, not cosmetic:** the holonomy group **correctly merges dependent cycles** — several nerve 1-cycles with the *same* holonomy contribute *one* generator, which the room-set proxy double-counted (capped at $\beta_1$). *Corroboration:* synthetic witness ⟹ $|G|=2$ ($G \cong \mathbb{Z}/2$, the $p\leftrightarrow q$ swap), e.a.2 ⟹ **rank $=1$ exact**; live data ⟹ $|G|=1$ ⟹ **rank $=0$**. To finish for a theorem: a paper proof that $\sigma_e$ is well-defined up to the spanning-tree choice (gauge) and that $\log_2|G|$ equals the $\check{C}$ech $H^1$ rank for the boolean coefficient (standard for a flat connection / local system on a graph). Agda-able with the T010 carrier.
2. **The identification (part 3).** Prove that "cycle of $N$ with non-trivial monodromy" $\iff$ "cycle outside $\mathcal{P}^\circ$ all of whose 2-cycle edges are in $\mathcal{P}^\circ$", directly from T010's $\mathcal{P}^\circ$ definition and the nerve's 1-cell condition. This is a definitional reduction once the rank argument is in hand — Agda-able alongside the T010 carrier. **✅ PROVEN on paper 2026-06-08** ([`C015-proof-rank-and-identification-2026-06-08.md`](../C015-proof-rank-and-identification-2026-06-08.md) Theorem B + Corollary B.1): the support clause + the local/global = off-nerve/non-trivial-monodromy dichotomy, reducing to T010's iso-monodromy characterization of $\mathcal{P}^\circ$. The **rank lemma** (Theorem A) and **gauge-independence** (Lemma 1) are proven in the same document. Pending non-author cross-check → then graduates as **T011**.
3. **A live witness (the empirical half).** Exhibit a non-trivial $H^1$ class on **real** `RoomFunctor` data (not the synthetic seed). Migrates the result from "construction validated" to "phenomenon observed", and would let C015 graduate to a theorem ($\to$ `02_THEOREMS_AND_PROOFS/`).

## Negative settlement (what a counterexample looks like, in what form)

- **(refutes part 1/3).** A non-trivial $H^1$ class carried by a cycle that is **not** globally obstructed in the §5 sense — e.g. a nerve cycle with non-trivial monodromy that is nonetheless *inside* $\mathcal{P}^\circ$ (would mean the ECC-iso oracle and the nerve 1-cell condition disagree), or a globally obstructed cycle that carries **no** class (would mean monodromy can be gauged away even with all edges compatible). Either decouples $H^1$ from the $\mathcal{P}^\circ$ partition.
- **(refutes part 2).** A *locally* obstructed cycle that carries a genuine $H^1$ class — i.e. a drop/non-faithful edge that is *not* removable by re-choosing the local section. Would mean local obstructions are **not** coboundaries and the local/global partition is not the $H^0/H^1$ split.
- **(empirical non-settlement, not a refutation).** Current live data has **no** global cell ($H^1=0$), so C015 cannot yet be *positively* settled on real data — only on the synthetic witness. This is a *vacuity-on-current-data* status (like B2b's abelian exactness), not a counterexample: it blocks graduation to a theorem until the corpus is richer, but does not refute the construction.

## Status of the empirical gap (honest)

The live Plexus is **too sparse** to exhibit contextuality *organically*: its only naturally-occurring out-of-$\mathcal{P}^\circ$ cycle is a **local drop** (coboundary-trivial), so the organic $H^1=0$. C015 is therefore **corroborated by construction** (the `cech` instrument returns the right answer on a genuine cell and on the all-local organic data) but **not yet observed in organic user activity**.

**Realistic witness (2026-06-08, `seed-real`).** Beyond the abstract `seed-contextuality` twist, a **substantively meaningful** witness was authored through the real schema: a **Condorcet cycle of evaluation framings** — three deliberation rooms evaluating two genuine rival instruments (Carbon Tax vs Cap-and-Trade) under cost-efficiency, political-durability, and distributional-equity criteria. Each pairwise translation is a *defensible* cross-framing ranking-equivalence; the loop reverses (equity vs efficiency), so there is **no consistent global ranking** — the canonical Arrow / discursive-dilemma disagreement. The `cech` pass returns **$H^1$ rank $=1$, holonomy group $\mathbb{Z}/2$** on it, with the organic local drop still correctly off-nerve. This shows the phenomenon is **real and meaningful, not an artifact of the abstract twist**: every claim is a genuine policy proposition and every functor map is a translation an analyst would author. It is, however, still **seeded** (authored to demonstrate, not emergent from independent user activity), so it strengthens the *construction* corroboration without closing the *organic-observation* rider.
