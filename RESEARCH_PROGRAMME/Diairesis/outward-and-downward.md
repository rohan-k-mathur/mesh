# Outward and Downward

*Two excavations from the first run of* The Priority of Distinction.
*They look like opposite moves — breadth versus depth. They are the same move twice,
because both are answers to the one thing the first run established: the master
question can't move because its disputed difference is* **sub-structural** *(I5),
so it reduces to a bedrock commitment (I6). Outward asks: is there a framing where
the difference is* not *sub-structural? Downward asks: is there a level where it is
self-evidencing? The filtered outward search and the bottom of the downward dig turn
out to be the same place.*

---

## Part A — Outward: the combinatorial frame-space

The first run used one frame: the thing/relation fork, three levels, three senses of
priority, posed globally, with a grounding-style priority relation. Every one of those
was a *choice*, and the space of alternative choices is almost entirely unoccupied.
The outward thread maps that space — but the map is worthless without a fitness
function, so the function comes first.

### A.1 The dimensions of a frame

A framing of "the priority of distinction" is a selection of one value from each of
six dimensions. The literature occupies a tiny, tight cluster; the rest is empty.

| Dim | What it fixes | Values (partial) | Where the literature sits |
|---|---|---|---|
| **D1 — the fork** | which pair is taken as fundamental | thing/relation · substance/process · being/becoming · identity/difference · one/many · content/form · presence/absence · figure/ground · marked/unmarked · determinate/indeterminate · actual/possible | thing/relation, substance/process |
| **D2 — the priority relation** | what "prior" means | grounding · temporal · generative/productive · logical · conceptual · explanatory · modal · mereological · constitutive | grounding, modal |
| **D3 — the direction** | which way it runs | A-prior · B-prior · co-primitive · neither (eliminate the fork) · the-relation-prior-to-both · no fixed direction (oscillating) | A-prior, B-prior, co-primitive |
| **D4 — the grain** | where it is asked | global · domain-local {logic, math, physics, computation, language, mind, normativity, perception} · uniform-vs-variable across domains | global |
| **D5 — modal status of the answer** | what kind of truth it is | necessary · contingent · conventional · indeterminate · self-evidencing/transcendental | necessary, contingent |
| **D6 — reflexivity** | does the frame apply to itself | non-reflexive · reflexive · self-undermining | non-reflexive |

The nominal grid is enormous and mostly degenerate, so its size is not the point. The
point is the *shape of the occupancy*: the entire received debate lives at roughly
⟨thing/relation or substance/process, grounding, {A,B,co-primitive}, global, {necessary,
contingent}, non-reflexive⟩. The first run lived there too — which is why it inherited
that region's fate (sub-structural difference, I6 bedrock).

### A.2 The fitness function (why most empty cells are junk)

Combinatorial spaces of framings are cheap; enumerating them is the §10 hazard
(taxonomy-as-progress) in pure form. A frame is worth occupying only if it can *move* —
and the first run tells us exactly what blocks movement: **a frame whose rival pictures
are isomorphic is stuck**, because the difference lives below the isomorphism ceiling
(I5) and no method indexes it. So the fitness function is:

> **Seek frames whose two pictures are *not* isomorphic — where one side has a feature
> the other structurally lacks.** Only a supra-structural difference can be moved by
> argument, construction, or observation.

This immediately kills most of the grid and lights up a specific region:

- **D2 = generative/temporal, not grounding.** "A *grounds* B" is the sub-structural,
  haecceity-shaped relation that sank the first run — two worlds can share all structure
  and differ only in what grounds what. But "A *produces* B" has a *dynamical* signature:
  a genesis, an order of appearance, a before/after that a structure can encode. Re-posed
  as a generative claim — *does distinguishing produce terms, as a process?* — the
  question acquires a temporal profile that is, in principle, structurally indexable.
- **D4 = domain-local with genuine genesis.** Domains that *develop* (computation as it
  runs, cognition as it learns, an organism as it differentiates) have an actual arrow of
  production. There the generative fork is not a frozen snapshot and the two pictures can
  come apart.
- **D6 = reflexive.** A frame that applies to itself can be *enacted*, and enactment is a
  supra-structural fact (it happens or it doesn't). This is the door to D5.
- **D5 = self-evidencing.** A frame whose truth is confirmed in the act of posing it sits
  entirely above the ceiling, because the confirming event is observable in the very
  positing.

### A.3 Result of the outward thread

The filtered search does not return a long list. It returns one region —
⟨generative, domain-local-with-genesis, reflexive, self-evidencing⟩ — and that region is
not a frame *about the world's joints* at all. It is a frame about *production-in-an-act*.
The outward search, run with the only fitness function the first run licenses, points away
from ontology and toward the act of distinguishing itself. Which is where the downward dig
independently arrives.

## Part B — Downward: the descent to the infrastructure

The downward thread does not look for a deeper *answer* to the master question. It follows
the *thrust behind* it — the drive to reach the generative bottom — and finds that the
thrust is enacted in the asking. Three levels.

### L0 — The ontological question (where the first run stopped)

*Is distinction prior to the distinguished, in being?* Bottoms at I6: the difference is
sub-structural, undecidable, a declared commitment. Pushing harder here yields nothing,
because the question is about the world's joints and the joints don't register the
difference.

### L−1 — The transcendental question (the first reframing)

Ask *why* L0 bottoms out — it asks about the world's structure — and pose the version that
doesn't: *Is distinction-drawing a condition of any positing, thinking, or theory
whatever?* This question is **self-evidencing**: it cannot be denied without being enacted,
since to deny it is to distinguish the denied from the affirmed. So unlike L0, it has a
demonstrable answer — and the answer is not conditional on primitive naturalness and not
sub-structural, because it is verified *performatively*, in the act.

Two things keep this from being a cheap win, and both are generative.

First, it is **substantive**, because it has a real opponent: *contentful but
undifferentiated apprehension* — pure presence with no cut (the non-dual / pure-consciousness
claim, Parmenidean being, the frame-level "self/world boundary dissolved" states the emotion
program's T5 targets). If such apprehension is possible, not all awareness presupposes
distinction. So L−1 sharpens to a question with a possible empirical-phenomenological floor:
*is distinctionless apprehension actual?* This reconnects, for the first time in the whole
inquiry, to something with a test.

Second, it **relocates rather than escapes the bedrock.** "Distinction is a condition of
thought" does not entail "distinction is prior in being" — that is the Kantian gap, and
crossing it is exactly the floor→ceiling laundering the spec forbids. So the discipline the
spec uses on others now binds the dig: *L−1 is floor; the ontological thesis is ceiling;
the dig may not derive the one from the other.* The bedrock has moved from "is there
sub-structural fact?" (L0) to "do conditions of thought constrain the structure of being?"
(the realism/idealism hinge) — an older, far more mapped bedrock, which is itself an advance.

### L−2 — The infrastructure (where the thrust arrives)

Go under L−1. It privileged "thinking/positing" — but why? Because the spec, the thinking,
the positing are all *activities*, and here the bottom shows itself: **an act and a
distinction are the same thing seen from two ends.** To act is to actualize this against
that — a cut. To distinguish is to do something — an act. Drawing-a-distinction and
acting-at-all are co-extensive.

If that identity holds, the master question *dissolves at this level rather than reducing*:
there is no fork to orient, because there are not two things (a relation and its terms,
a thing and its distinctions) to put in order. There is one **self-differentiating process**
whose "sides" — marked/unmarked, before/after, term/relation — are its own phases, not
independent relata. The fork was an artifact of taking a *snapshot* of the process and
asking which frozen side came first. The priority question is what you get when you freeze a
verb and interrogate the still.

This is the infrastructure of the scaffolding, in the literal sense the thread was after:
**the spec is built out of distinctions, so the spec is a working instance of its own
subject.** The first run read that reflexivity as a defect (I3 self-undermining recursion;
I5 the formalism going blind to its own object). The dig reads the identical fact as the
*resource*: precisely because the spec runs on distinction-drawing, distinction-in-action is
present *in the method*, not only in the object — and you can study the primitive by studying
the operation that builds the scaffolding. The scaffolding is the experiment. The thrust
behind the question — the search for a generative source — finds the source operating in the
hand that writes the question.

### B.1 The generative question, and why it escapes I6

The dissolution at L−2 is not an ending; it specifies a different question that *is*
generative:

> **What is the minimal self-differentiating operation, and what does it generate under
> iteration?**

This escapes the I6 bedrock because it is no longer asking *is there a hidden fact?* but
*what does this operation produce?* — which is answerable by **construction**. You exhibit a
candidate operation (Spencer-Brown's re-entry of the mark into its own form; a categorical
fixed point / initial algebra; the lambda calculus's self-application; the autopoietic loop),
run it, and check what falls out: does it generate the numerals? the level-structure
(term/relation/act) the spec assumed? the marked/unmarked asymmetry? A candidate that
generates the scaffolding's own apparatus from a single self-applying cut would be the thing
the inquiry was reaching for — and it would have a falsification surface, because a candidate
that generates nothing, or that smuggles its products in, fails visibly.

So the downward dig **hands off to a constructive program with a test** — the first floor the
metaphysical inquiry has touched since the epistemic axis went inert.

## Part C — Where they meet, and what it costs

**The convergence.** The outward search, filtered by the only fitness function the first run
licenses, points at ⟨generative, domain-local-with-genesis, reflexive, self-evidencing⟩ — a
frame about production-in-an-act, not about the world's joints. The downward dig, following
the thrust to the bottom, arrives at the self-differentiating operation — production-in-an-act
made the primitive. These are the same destination reached from opposite directions. That is
the real result of this turn: breadth and depth were never two threads; they were two routes
to the reframing that converts an undecidable ontological question into a constructive
operational one.

**What it costs (the honest debits).**

*The master question is not answered; it is abandoned as the wrong push.* The dig does not
excavate a deeper answer to "is distinction prior in being?" — that stays at I6 exactly where
the first run left it. It finds an *adjacent* question that is generative and shows the
original was posed at the wrong level. That is a genuine finding, but it is partly
deflationary about the whole enterprise: the scaffolding was built to ask a question its own
infrastructure turns out to answer better by not asking.

*The outward thread is parasitic on the inward one.* Without the supra-structurality filter,
the frame-space is just taxonomy — and the filter comes entirely from the first run's I5
diagnosis. So the breadth move has no independent standing; it earns its keep only as a way of
confirming, from the outside, where the depth move lands. Strictly, the inward thread is prior
— which is a priority claim, and the irony is noted.

*The generative question inherits the Q-012 risk in a new costume.* "The minimal
self-differentiating operation" may be a determinate object or it may be a *verb* wearing a
crown — the processual reprise of the "preposition wearing a crown" the essays already flagged.
The position is a promissory note, redeemable only by exhibiting an operation that does real
generative work without smuggling. If no construction redeems it, L−2 is not a bottom but a
restatement, and the dig collapses back toward I6. The test is the construction, and until the
construction runs the generative claim is a hypothesis, not a result. This is the right kind of
risk — it has a falsification surface — but it is a risk, and the program should not bank L−2
until a construction pays for it.

## Next moves (each now has a real handle)

- **Constructive (highest yield).** Take one candidate self-differentiating operation —
  re-entry is the most worked-out — and test whether it generates the scaffolding's own
  apparatus (numerals; the term/relation/act levels; the marked/unmarked asymmetry) without
  smuggling. Success redeems L−2; failure routes back to I6. This is the only move in the
  whole inquiry with a genuine pass/fail.
- **Phenomenological (reconnects L−1 to a floor).** Pursue *is distinctionless apprehension
  actual?* against the frame-level instruments (the EASE/EAWE-style and contemplative-report
  literature the emotion program already inventories), keeping the floor/ceiling guard: a
  positive finding bounds the transcendental claim, it does not settle the ontological one.
- **Cartographic (lowest yield, do last).** Fill the high-value cells of the frame-space as a
  cross-check on the convergence — but only the supra-structural region, and only to confirm it
  points where the dig arrived. Enumerating the rest is the hazard the format exists to refuse.
