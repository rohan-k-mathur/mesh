**Instance.** Extend the aspirin base ([Q-027](q027-thin-cones-2026-05-29.md) ¬ß1) with a

## S2 ‚ÄĒ the sweep

### Test 1 ‚ÄĒ head-action / daimon-lock (E2 Test 1, verbatim mechanism)

Each material proof design's **first positive action** is the top rule it selects, pinned
in the thin cone by the Daimon Lock ([Q-027](q027-thin-cones-2026-05-29.md) ¬ß5.2). This
partitions both sides by head rule:

| head rule | `Gen!(B)` class | `ūĚíě_base` class |
|---|---|---|
| `t‚āĀ` | `{D_{t‚āĀ}}` | `{a‚āĀ}` |
| `t‚āÉ` | `{D_{t‚āÉ}}` | `{a‚āÉ}` |
| `chk` | `{D_{chk/t‚āĀ}, D_{chk/t‚āÉ}}` | `{b‚āĀ, b‚āÉ}` |

Any head-respecting bijection preserves this partition: the two **singletons force**
`D_{t‚āĀ}‚Ü¶a‚āĀ`, `D_{t‚āÉ}‚Ü¶a‚āÉ`, and the head-`chk` block maps to itself (`2!` ways). So **Test 1
prunes 24 ‚Üí 2 survivors:** `ŌÜ` (diagonal) and `ŌÉ` (the **scheme swap**:
`D_{chk/t‚āĀ}‚Ü¶b‚āÉ`, `D_{chk/t‚āÉ}‚Ü¶b‚āĀ`). Unlike stratum-1, Test 1 leaves a **higher-order**
2-block, not a propositional one.

### Test 2 ‚ÄĒ deterministic-proof-search compatibility at the `!`-scheme locus (THE STRATUM-2 NOVELTY)

`ŌÜ` and `ŌÉ` agree on `D_{t‚āĀ}, D_{t‚āÉ}` and differ only on the two `chk`-generators; the
discriminator is the **head of the abstracted derivation in the `!`-slot**. This is the
higher-order analogue of E2 Test 2 (which probed a first-order *premise* locus, the `¬¨gu`
sub-chronicle); here the probe descends **into the exponential/contraction structure**:

- **Ludics side.** `D_{chk/t‚āĀ}`'s `!`-slot sub-design has head action `t‚āĀ`;
  `D_{chk/t‚āÉ}`'s has head `t‚āÉ`. A counter-design of `B‚ä•` that **drives `chk`'s `!`-argument
  and reads its head action** converges with **exactly** `D_{chk/t‚āÉ}` when probing for
  `t‚āÉ` (and `D_{chk/t‚āĀ}` for `t‚āĀ`). This descent is precisely **BT2010 ¬ß3.1 deterministic
  proof search** (T012's read-back): *"the head variable `z` and the first positive action
  `a` completely determine the next ‚Ä¶ rule"* ‚ÄĒ applied recursively into the `!`-slot, it
  reads the scheme's head **single-valuedly**. Materiality (Res-C) guarantees the scheme
  sub-design is **visited/load-bearing** (`chk` uses it), so its head is probeable, not
  immaterial.
- **Ambler side.** The term whose abstracted scheme is `t‚āÉ` is **exactly** `b‚āÉ =
  chk‚ü®!t‚āÉ, m‚ü©`.
- **Forcing.** Composition compatibility (the bridge commutes with cutting against the
  `t‚āÉ`-probe) **forces** `D_{chk/t‚āÉ}‚Ü¶b‚āÉ`, hence `D_{chk/t‚āĀ}‚Ü¶b‚āĀ` = `ŌÜ`. The swap `ŌÉ` sends
  `D_{chk/t‚āÉ}‚Ü¶b‚āĀ` (whose abstracted scheme is `t‚āĀ`, not `t‚āÉ`), so it **fails to commute**
  with the `t‚āÉ`-probe. **`ŌÉ` is eliminated.**

> **Why the contraction does not muddy it.** `chk` uses its scheme **twice**, so the
> `!`-slot sub-design appears at two positive loci ‚ÄĒ but **both copies have the same head**
> (`t‚āĀ`, resp. `t‚āÉ`): they are copies of one abstracted derivation. So the probe reads the
> same scheme head whichever copy it drives; the contraction adds multiplicity, not
> ambiguity. This is the `!`-layer-specific point that only became checkable once T012's
> deterministic read-back was established.

### Test 3 ‚ÄĒ U-erasure compatibility (E2 Test 3, verbatim)

underlying generators, so U-erasure is **non-discriminating** ‚ÄĒ it adds nothing beyond
cardinality (already matched by T012). `ŌÜ` passes trivially.
