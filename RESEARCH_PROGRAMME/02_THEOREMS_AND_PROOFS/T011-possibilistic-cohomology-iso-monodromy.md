## Vocabulary

Fix a tracked proposition. Work over the **strict ECC iso**: in the quotient `ECC/≈` (T010 D1 §0), two claims `x, x'` in a room `r` are **strict-iso** (`x ≈_r x'`) iff there are ECC arrows `x → x'` and `x' → x` whose round-trip composites are **`isIsoVia`-invertible** — assumption-free identities in `ECC/≈` (`isIsoVia(fwd,back) = isIdentityArrow(compose(back,fwd)) ∧ isIdentityArrow(compose(fwd,back))`, `isIdentityArrow` = equal endpoints, one derivation, empty net assumption union). This is **strictly finer** than the probe's claim-level two-way reachability `interDerivable` (which ignores assumptions); the probe is corroboration only.

The **compatible nerve** `N` (a graph / 1-skeleton): 0-cells = rooms appearing in a compatible pair; **1-cells** = unordered room pairs `{r,s}` whose transport functors `r→s`, `s→r` exist and whose back-edge 2-cycle is **`isIsoVia`-invertible** (the pair is in **𝓟°** as a 2-cycle, T010). The **fiber** `F_r` = strict-iso-classes of tracked claims in `r`; `F_r^∘` = the sub-fiber surviving transport. **Holonomy** of a fundamental cycle (spanning tree `T`, root `r₀`, non-tree edge `e`): `σ_e = t_v^{-1} ∘ τ_{uv} ∘ t_u ∈ Sym(F_{r₀}^∘)`; **holonomy group** `G = ⟨σ_e : e ∉ T⟩`. Possibilistic (boolean / min-quantale) coefficient: `H⁰` = global sections = `Fix(G)`; `H¹` = obstruction to gluing pairwise-compatible local data.

## Supporting lemmas

- **Lemma 0 (edges carry fiber isomorphisms — the local system).** Each 1-cell `{r,s} ∈ E(N)` induces a bijection `τ_{rs}: F_r^∘ → F_s^∘` with `τ_{sr} = τ_{rs}^{-1}`. So `(N, {τ})` is a **flat local system** on the graph. *Proof rests on exactly two ingredients, neither the proxy:* (i) `transport` descends to strict-iso-classes because it is a **strict 1-functor on `ECC/≈` that adds no assumptions** (T010 Lemma L1; `transport` copies the assumption map verbatim), so it carries an assumption-free round-trip identity to an assumption-free round-trip identity; (ii) the back-edge round trip is the identity on `F_r^∘` because the **strict 𝓟°-2-cycle edge condition** says exactly that it is `isIsoVia`-invertible.
- **Lemma 1 (gauge-independence).** `G` is independent of the spanning tree and basepoint up to conjugacy in `Sym(F^∘)`; hence `|G|` is a well-defined invariant. *Proof:* the classical flat-bundle fact — `π₁(N,r₀)` is free on the fundamental cycles; a tree/basepoint change is an inner automorphism of `π₁` composed with conjugation by the tree-transport permutation, leaving the image's conjugacy class (hence `|G|`) fixed; survives the partial-bijection (claims-that-drop) refinement on the surviving sub-fiber.

## Statement

> **T011.** For the Plexus claim-sheaf over the compatible nerve `N` (1-cells = strict-`isIsoVia` 𝓟°-2-cycles), with the local system of Lemma 0 and holonomy group `G`, `β₁ = |E(N)| − |V(N)| + 1` per component:
>
> 1. **(Rank lemma — Theorem A.)**
>    - (1) The possibilistic `H¹` obstruction is non-trivial **iff `G ≠ 1`** (`H⁰ = Fix(G)`, Corollary 1.1).
>    - (2) When `G` is an **elementary abelian 2-group** (`G ≅ (ℤ/2)^k`, every `σ_e` an involution — the binary drift-iso/twist case), the obstruction is classified by `ρ̄: H₁(N;𝔽₂) → G` and its **rank is exactly**
>      `rank H¹ = log₂|G| = β₁ − dim_{𝔽₂} ker ρ̄`.
>    - (3) For general (non-involution / non-abelian) `G`, `⌊log₂|G|⌋` is a **lower bound** (the construction flags `elementaryAbelian2 = false`); exactness is **never** asserted there.
> 2. **(Identification — Theorem B + Corollary B.1.)** A 1-cycle `γ` of `N` carries **non-trivial monodromy** (round-trip not the identity on `F^∘`) **iff** `γ` is **outside 𝓟°** while **every consecutive room-pair is in 𝓟° as a 2-cycle** (pairwise-in, globally-out). Hence `H¹` is supported **exactly** on the globally-obstructed cycles, and a **locally** obstructed cycle (a drop or a non-𝓟° pair — an edge that is *not* a 1-cell of `N`) is **not** in `Z₁(N)` and carries **no** `H¹` class (coboundary-trivial). Consequently the **Abramsky contextuality obstruction** of distributed deliberation — pairwise-consistent local sections that do not glue — **is** T010's iso-monodromy obstruction.

## What this rules out (for the implementation)

- **No claim-id coherence checks for the nerve.** A 1-cell of `N` (and the fiber quotient) must be tested by **strict ECC inter-derivability up to `isIsoVia`** (assumption-free round-trip), **not** claim-id equality and **not** the probe's assumption-ignoring `interDerivable` — else the nerve and the reported `H¹` rank are over-counted (the D1 failure). The probe's `cech` numbers are an **upper bound**; trust them as exact only where round-trips are assumption-free (the binary witnesses).
- **No exact rank off the e.a.2 case.** Any surface reporting `rank H¹ = log₂|G|` as exact must gate on `elementaryAbelian2 = true`; for non-involution / non-abelian holonomy it is a lower bound only.
- **No "global disagreement" claim from a local obstruction.** A drop or a non-𝓟° pair has a **locus** (Direction 2 / [T008](T008-minimal-separating-context-daimon-closed.md)) and carries **no** `H¹` class; only a pairwise-in / globally-out nerve cycle is a genuine global (Abramsky-contextual) obstruction.
- **No live-class claim.** This theorem is *about the construction*; asserting an organic non-trivial `H¹` on real data requires the Q-043(iii) witness (or an explicit methodology call to accept the synthetic/realistic seed), which this does not supply.
